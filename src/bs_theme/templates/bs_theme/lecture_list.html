Caesar Cipher
  • Used by Julius Caesar around 50 BC for military communications
  • It is a shift cipher where letters are rotated to a certain degree based on the key. The key tells how many letters to shift to create the cipher text
  • ROT 13 is a widely known cipher using the same algortithm as the Caesar Cipher, but only allows shift of 13
  • Can be broken easily with word analysis. Think about the frequency of each letter used in our language. Hint: Vowels are highest.
